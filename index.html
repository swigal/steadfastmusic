Steadfast Music Web App

I have made a big update to our website. I have configured it to allow you to properly add it to your mobile device just as you would any other app. Depending on whether you are on and android or iOS device, you may need to do a few extra steps to get it setup. Simply elect the your device below for instructions to install the web app.

I have also setup push notifications. So make sure to enable those when you receive the prompt so you will be notified of future updates on the website.

iOS (iPhone, iPad) Instructions

Step 1.

Open Safari (or your preferred web browser) on your iPhone or iPad.

Step 2.

Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow coming out of it).

Step 3.

Select "Add to Home Screen" from the menu that appears.

Step 4.

Tap "Add" to confirm and add the web app to your home screen.

Android Instructions

Step 1.

Open Chrome (or your preferred web browser) on your Android device.

Step 2.

Tap the three dots in the top right corner of the browser window.

Step 3.

Select "Add to Home Screen" from the menu that appears.

Step 4.

Tap "Add" to confirm and add the web app to your home screen.

Important Note:

You should find the web app icon in your list of installed apps. You may have to manually move it to the home screen if it doesn't appear there automatically.

Nothing But the Blood - Song Devotional

This morning I just finished listening to yesterdays "Sing" devotion with the song "Nothing But the Blood." Sing is a daily devotion prouced by The Worship Initiative. The Worship Initiative is a ministry that seeks to Equippe and encourage worship teams as they sing the truth of God’s word and glorify Christ. THey created "Sing" as another means of forwarding this mission. Every weekday they sit down and have a time of prayer, devotion and worship, focusing on one song each day.

Friday's devotion was on the song "Nothing But the Blood" by The Worship Initiative. The devotion focused on how this song reminds us of the sacrifice of Christ and how we are saved by His grace alone. Even though we try to turn to things that seem to satisfy us for a moment, we are reminded that only the blood of Christ can wash away sin. "This is all our hope and peace. Nothing But the Blood of Jesus."

I wanted to encourage you guys to try out Sing. I may not listen to it every day, but the ones I have listened to have been incredible. Sometimes they chose an old hymn, sometimes its an original contemporary song. But the devotions are always simple acoustic, so no drums and electric guitars. Just piano, acoustic guitar and focusing on God's presence in our lives.

If you want to check out the devotion, follow this link sing.theworshipinitiative.com.

7 Characteristics of a Worship Leader

Becoming an effective worship leader requires developing several key characteristics. Here are the seven essential traits every worship leader should strive for:

  1. A Heart for God: Prioritize a personal relationship with God and allow that to fuel your worship leading.
  2. A Heart for People: Lead with love, serve your team and congregation, and build a strong community.
  3. Humility: Be humble and avoid pride.
  4. Knowledge of God’s Word: Have a solid understanding of the Bible and theology.
  5. Organization/Administration Skills: Manage your team well and plan services thoughtfully.
  6. Communication Skills: Communicate clearly and genuinely, both verbally and non-verbally. Be teachable and learn to listen more.
  7. Musical Ability: Develop your skills in order to be the most effective you can be in the musical aspect of worship.

These characteristics are crucial for effective worship leadership. Aim to embody them in your ministry, always seeking growth and improvement.

Embrace these principles, and you’ll be well on your way to becoming a truly great worship leader.
